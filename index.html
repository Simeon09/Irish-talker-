Welcome to The Talk Show for Talkers

The Talk Show for Talkers is a Podcast covering everything to do with public speaking, confidence and leadership. It is published in every Saturday as a podcast on Anchor, iTunes and other podcasting platforms as well as on this website.

The show is hosted by experienced Toastmasters, Paul O'Mahony, James Finnegan and Moira O'Brien. First broadcast in November 2014, the Talk Show is now in its 9th season with over 360 shows (as of January 2023) and is listened to in at least 80 countries with Podcast downloads exceeding 166,000 (as of January 2023).
